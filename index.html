About Us

To create an atmosphere conducive for conduct of contemporary and up-to-date teaching – learning activities wherein new ideas or innovations in the field of cutting age research and technologies are pitched and conceptualized. To impart total quality engineering education to develop technically competent, professionally superior and ethical workforce fit for globally competitive environment. To provide a platform that transforms students through rigorous coursework, hands-on practice and skill training to emerge as socially responsible engineer cum entrepreneur who can understand and meet the needs of the society and industry. To encourage student-faculty linkage with industry, academia and research institutions of eminence through projects that address to the common problems faced by the nation and the world

Euphoria

A dance club

Discription

A college dance club is a vibrant and energetic group within a college or university where students come together to express themselves through various forms of dance. It serves as a creative outlet for students who are passionate about dancing, regardless of their skill level. Members typically engage in a wide range of dance styles, such as hip-hop, contemporary, classical, salsa, and more, allowing them to explore different genres and techniques. The club often organizes regular practice sessions, workshops, and dance battles to help members hone their skills. They may also participate in college events, inter-college competitions, and cultural festivals, showcasing their talent and representing their college. The club fosters a sense of community and teamwork, encouraging collaboration among dancers and providing a supportive environment for creative expression. In addition to technical skills, the dance club emphasizes the importance of fitness, discipline, and creativity, helping students grow both as dancers and individuals. It is a place where students can build friendships, gain confidence, and experience the joy of performing in front of an audience.

Lekhni

A Poetry and Story Telling club

Discription

A college poetry club is a sanctuary for students who are passionate about the written word and the art of expression. It is a creative community where poets, writers, and lovers of literature come together to share their thoughts, emotions, and ideas through poetry. The club is a space for students to explore the power of language, experiment with different styles of writing, and connect with others who share a similar passion.

Members of the poetry club often participate in activities such as writing workshops, open-mic nights, poetry slams, and collaborative writing sessions. These events provide opportunities for members to refine their craft, gain feedback from peers, and build confidence in their writing abilities. The club may also organize readings, where members can showcase their work to an audience, fostering a sense of accomplishment and community.

The poetry club encourages creativity, self-expression, and introspection, allowing members to delve into personal experiences, social issues, and imaginative worlds through their writing. It is a space where every voice is valued, and diverse perspectives are celebrated. In this club, students can develop their literary skills, form meaningful connections with others, and discover the profound impact of poetry on both the individual and collective levels.
